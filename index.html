Our Story

The Blue Light – Motheo Wa Tumelo Gospel Choir began as a church choir under The Saviour Department Apostolic Church in South Africa.

On 16 September 2016, the choir was renamed Motheo Wa Tumelo Gospel Choir to become a community choir. This change was made to welcome people from outside the church, as many had grown to love the choir and were eager to join, but were previously unable to do so while it operated strictly as a church choir.

Since then, the choir has continued to grow, bringing together people from different backgrounds who share a passion for gospel music, faith, and unity.

In 2026, the choir reached a major milestone with the release of its album “HA KE HOPOLA WENA”, which was officially launched on 28 March 2026.

We are a passionate choir performing at events and uplifting communities through music.
